# Autonomous Innovation Governance Lane Objective

## Primary Goals
- Establish and maintain comprehensive governance for autonomous innovation
- Define clear experimental protocols
- Manage risk and quality control for autonomous experiments

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Hypothesis clarity and measurability
- Experiment pass/fail rate
- Risk mitigation effectiveness
- Governance contract compliance

## Scope
- Develop and evolve innovation governance framework
- Create templates for experimental protocols
- Implement systematic review and validation processes

## Continuous Improvement
- Quarterly review of governance contract
- Adaptation to emerging autonomous innovation challenges
- Feedback loop from experimental outcomes
